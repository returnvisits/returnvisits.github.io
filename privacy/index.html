Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026
The short version: Field Service Companion stores your data on your device, encrypted. We don't have access to your contacts, visit notes, or personal information. We collect nothing unless you explicitly opt in.

Who we are

Field Service Companion ("FSC", "the app") is developed by Return Visits, based in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. You can reach us at hello@returnvisits.org.

What data the app accesses

Data Purpose Storage
Contacts you create in FSC Core app functionality — managing return visits Device only
Visit notes & literature records Logging conversations and placed literature Device only
Device contacts (READ_CONTACTS) Optional import — only accessed when you choose to import a contact Device only
Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) Show your current position on the contact map Device only
Encrypted backups Optional backup you create and control with a recovery phrase Device only

What we do NOT collect

FSC version 1.x has no server component. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on our servers. Specifically:

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No crash reporting sent to us
  • No contact data leaves your device
  • No location data leaves your device

Device permissions

Contacts (READ_CONTACTS) — requested only when you use the "Import Contact" feature. FSC reads the selected contact's name and address to create a new FSC record. No contact data is transmitted off your device.

Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) — requested only when you open the Map view. Your location is used to display your position on the map alongside your FSC contacts. Location data never leaves your device.

Encryption

All FSC data stored on your device is encrypted using industry-standard algorithms. Backups are encrypted with XChaCha20-Poly1305 and protected by a recovery phrase that only you know. We cannot access or recover your data.

Third-party services

FSC v1.x uses Google Maps SDK to display the contact map. Google's use of location data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. No other third-party services receive your data.

Children's privacy

FSC is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Future versions

When FSC introduces cloud sync and optional telemetry in future versions, this policy will be updated before those features launch. Cloud features will always be opt-in and end-to-end encrypted.

Changes to this policy

We will update this page when the policy changes. Material changes will be communicated through the app before they take effect.

Contact us

Questions or concerns about this privacy policy? Email us at hello@returnvisits.org.
